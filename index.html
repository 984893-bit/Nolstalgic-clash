features of the game 

 

what the features looks like

 

  Nostalgic Clash is a fast-paced, 2D arcade sports brawler built on Scratch that shifts the focus from giant bobble-heads to high-flying, full-body athleticism. Players control retro-inspired fighters who utilize dynamic, multi-frame costume animations to execute acrobatic moves like aerial bicycle kicks, diving headers, and ground-sweeping slide tackles to outmaneuver their opponent. Powered by precise grid variables tracking X and Y velocities, the game features a bouncy, chaotic physics engine where the ball reacts realistically to different body strikes. The defining feature is the literal "Clash" system—when both players attempt a full-body strike simultaneously, the game triggers a frantic button-mashing event that sends the loser flying into the net and the winner blasting a fiery power shot. Wrapped in a charming 8-bit aesthetic complete with screen-shaking particle trails, color-flashing hit indicators, and retro chiptunes, this Scratch project delivers intense 1v1 action that rewards perfect timing and positioning.
